A Tale Of Two Blogs

I have two blogs currently. Both I write under the psuedonym, Sonja Sol

One blog is a personal blog, Tranny In A Can, which focuses on the two ways I seek to live a fulfilling life:
 --Living Authentically
 --Living Deliberately

Respectively, these focus on:
 --Exploring the world outside of gender norms and otherwise resisting the itch to be socially acceptable
 --Getting out of the box of mindless living, looking at new ways to live, contemplating novel ways to make money and attain resources

The other is a religously oriented blog, Eye On The Horizon, which is a comparative study of world religions with an eye on the future of religion in a rapidly changing world.
