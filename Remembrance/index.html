What is Remembrance?

Remembrance is an analysis of the HBO series, Game of Thrones. Largely a work of comparative religion, it draws from the rich heritage of world religions, mythology, Alchemy, and Jungian Psychology to dig up a message underlying the surface of the symbol-rich epic. It is currently in the works. The plan is to have 12 main articles, one introductory, and several supplementary articles, all online, with the possibility of an ebook version. Some artwork and video clips will be included. Each of the main articles will have its own subject matter, but taken together form a sequence of steps from the claim to the conclusion. For more on the nature of the project and the claim it makes, read the Introduction article linked below.

What is the Purpose of this Preview?

This preview was made with the aim of garnering financial support and anticipation for the Remembrance project which may take several more months or more than a year to finish in addition to the many months that have already been spent.

Remembrance will be the flagship product of Idol Word, a fledgling sole proprietorship, a blog and ebook publishing company focusing on general religion, a religious praise of language and the art which it makes possible. As the sole writer/researcher currently working on it, I, the author of this page, hope to give the Remembrance project the full-time devotion it deserves and to get Idol Word to where it grows funds enough to live on. As a work, I consider the Remembrance project to be more valuable, more a contribution to society than what I currently do at a part-time job working for the bare necessities.

A link for contributing follows the preview. If you would like to volunteer time to assist the author or just message me, the sole writer/researcher, a link will be below also.
