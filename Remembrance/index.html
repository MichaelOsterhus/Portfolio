What is Remembrance?

Remembrance is to be an analysis of the HBO series, Game of Thrones. On the presumption that the story is a religious parable, the analysis will leverage the hefty heritage of world religions, mythology, Alchemy, and Jungian Psychology to pry up a message underlying the planks of the symbol-rich epic.

It is currently in the works.

12 main articles have been outlined, one introductory article written, and several supplementary articles presupposed. All to be published online, with the possibility of an ebook version. Some artwork and video clips will be made also.

What is the Purpose of this Preview?

This preview was made with the aim of garnering financial support and anticipation for the Remembrance project.

A link for contributing follows the preview.
